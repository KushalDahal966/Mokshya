About Mokshya

Every evening turns into a carnival

Mokshya, in English, means The Success which stands true as Mokshya Garden is the synonym for celebration of life itself. Embraced by lush greenery, the aesthetic setup stood as Nature welcomed you with open arms and delicious flavors.

From contemplating morning coffee to vibrant evening glass of cocktail, from cozy quiet corners for quiet moments to lively gatherings under the stars with crackling bonfires, we have got the perfect setting for any mood, accompanied by soul-soothing live music.

And the best part? We are just a leisurely ten-minute stroll from Mukti Chowk, Birtamode.
