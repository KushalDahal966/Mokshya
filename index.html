ABOUT MOKSHYA

Every evening turns into a carnival

Mokshya, in English, means The Success which stands true as Mokshya Garden is the synonym for celebration of life itself.

From morning coffee to vibrant cocktails, from cozy corners to lively gatherings under the stars, we have the perfect setting for any mood.

And the best part? We are just a leisurely ten-minute stroll from Mukti Chowk, Birtamode.
